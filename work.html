Graphic Design

Here's a collection of some of my favorite graphic design work I've done.

AMAZON — Logo Design.

■ PROJECT: Fleet Utilization Analyzer
■ ROLE: Logo Design

I was hired by an a team of developers at Amazon to design a logo for a piece of software they created for internal use. The process was really fun and the client and I landed on a common direction that helped accelerate the process.

Facebook — Logo Design.

■ PROJECT: Rate My Bot AI Chatbot
■ ROLE: Logo Design

I was hired by Facebook to make logos for an AI chatbot they were internally demo-ing. The process timeline was very short consisting of less than 1 week for the full project turn-around. Thankfully, the client had a strong vision for what they wanted which helped guide my work.

VHHC — Company Identity.

■ PROJECT: VHHC
■ ROLE: Logo, Document Header, and Business Card Design

I was hired by VHHC to re-design their company identity. We started with their logos which we tried wordmarks, abstractmarks, and a few lettermarks before landing on the shaking hands 'V' symbol. After some iterating the client selected the final logo which you can see above.

El Camino College — Logo Design.

■ PROJECT: ASO Logo Design
■ ROLE: Logo Design & Print Design

I was hired by El Camino College's ASO to re-design their logo. The client requested something modern and minimal that could be applied like a seal. I relied a lot on flat design and a strong balanced typeface to create the final product

Associated Students of Berkeley City College — Logo Design.

■ ROLE: Logo Design & Print Design
■ DEMO: visit semplice.com
